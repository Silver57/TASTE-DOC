\section{AI Usage Disclosure}
\label{app:ai}

\todo{Per the Nova SBE Generative AI guidelines, declare here, honestly and
specifically:

\textbf{Tools used.} List by name (e.g.\ ChatGPT, Claude) and approximate
period of use.

\textbf{How they were used.} Distinguish across the three guideline
categories: (a) information retrieval -- which queries, what was returned,
where in the thesis that information appears (cite each as personal
communication, with date); (b) content generation -- if any text was
AI-drafted and then edited by the author, describe the workflow and date
of access; (c) editing assistance -- proofreading, rephrasing, formatting
help, which the guidelines explicitly do not require disclosure for, but
which is good practice to mention briefly anyway.

\textbf{What AI was NOT used for.} Be explicit: the experimental design,
the running of the sweeps, the analysis of the results, and the defense of
the methodological and business arguments are the author's own work. Per
the guidelines, the responsibility to discuss, defend, and justify the
content of the thesis remains solely with the student.

\textbf{Citation practice.} Any specific factual content sourced from an AI
tool is cited in-text as personal communication on the relevant page, in
line with the guideline.}
